# 09 — AI Effects, Color Grading & Audio

## Goal
AI-powered visual effects, automatic color grading, and intelligent audio enhancement.

---

## Tasks

### 9.1 AI Color Grading
- [ ] Auto white-balance correction
- [ ] Match color between clips (consistency across scenes)
- [ ] Style transfer: "Make it look like [movie/mood]"
- [ ] LUT generation from reference images
- [ ] Before/after comparison slider
- [ ] Preset library: cinematic, vintage, neon, warm, cold, noir

### 9.2 AI Video Enhancement
- [ ] Auto stabilization (deshake)
- [ ] Upscaling (720p → 1080p via AI super-resolution)
- [ ] Noise reduction (low-light footage cleanup)
- [ ] Auto-crop for different aspect ratios (16:9 → 9:16 with smart framing)
- [ ] Background blur / bokeh effect
- [ ] AI background removal (green screen without green screen)

### 9.3 AI Audio
- [ ] Auto audio leveling (normalize volume)
- [ ] Background noise removal
- [ ] Echo/reverb reduction
- [ ] AI voice enhancement (make speech clearer)
- [ ] Auto-ducking (lower music when speech starts)
- [ ] AI music suggestions based on video mood
- [ ] Royalty-free music library integration
- [ ] AI sound effects matching (optional)

### 9.4 AI-Generated Assets
- [ ] AI thumbnail generation (best frame + text overlay)
- [ ] AI title card generation
- [ ] AI b-roll suggestions (stock footage matching your content)
- [ ] AI voiceover generation (text-to-speech for narration)
